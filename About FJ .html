    Faith Wisalth is a pastor, prophet, and author based in Accra, Ghana. He is the founder and Head Pastor of Words of Eternal Life Ministries.

    Joseph Faith Wisalth was born on July 23, 1987 in Konongo to Kate Agyei and James Asiamah Mensah. He attended Abuakwa State College and went to the University of Cape Coast where he graduated in 2010.

    His Call and Mandate.

    While going through LUKE 18:1-8, I read Jesus say, ‘WHEN THE SON OF MAN SHALL RETURN, SHALL HE FIND FAITH IN THE EARTH?’

    Upon reading this, I realized that THE LORD WOULD WANT HIS PEOPLE TO KEEP ON WALKING IN FAITH TILL HE RETURNS. So, immediately I said, ‘LORD, USE ME TO PRESERVE FAITH IN YOUR CHURCH TILL YOU COME’.

    So, I knew I was called to PREACH AND TEACH THE WORD OF FAITH.

    Another time, the Lord led me to spend some time with Him. At the course of the fellowship, He led me to Numbers 13 and 14.

    When I got to verse 28 which reads, ‘Say unto them, As truly as I live, saith the LORD, as ye have spoken in mine ears, so will I do to you:’, I realized this strong word in my spirit that FAITH IS ABOUT WHAT YOU BELIEVE AND KEEP SAYING. And when that happens, you will get whatever you have been saying.

    The Lord by these scriptures, I believe, gave me the lead to PREACH AND TEACH THE WORD OF FAITH.

    But I had always knew that THE WORD OF FAITH must have A CONTENT to be preached and taught. That is, WHAT AM I TO PREACH AND TEACH WHICH WOULD BE CALLED THE WORD OF FAITH?

    After a while, I had the Holy Spirit began to expatiate to me this scripture, JOHN 3:16. And I realized His emphasis was on the words EVERLASTING LIFE.

    From that day, HE REVEALED TO ME that this EVERLASTING LIFE was THE LIFE OF GOD which He seeks that ALL MEN WOULD HAVE AND WALK IN. telling me that THIS WAS THE CONTENT OF THE WORD OF FAITH TO BE PREACHED AND TAUGHT.

    Therefore, do we (the Holy Spirit and I) preach and teach men TO HAVE AND LIVE THE LIFE OF GOD so as to see all men TO LIVE AS GOD IN THE EARTH.

    And this was going to be possible by BELIEVING THAT JESUS IS THE SON OF GOD. Thereby, ONE ALSO BECOMES GOD’S SON WITH HIS LIFE IN HIM. JOHN 1:10-13
