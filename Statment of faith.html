STATEMENT OF FAITH

  • The Bible - 2 Timothy 3:16, Joshua 1:8, Psalm 1:1, 2
  • All men have sinned - Romans 3:23, Romans 5:12
  • The only way to God is Jesus Christ - John 14:6, John 10:9
  • TGod in Three Persons (The Trinity) - Colossians 2:9, Matthew 28:19
  • Heaven and Hell (The final Destination of the righteous and the Sinner respectively) - 1 Thessalonians 4:16, 17; Matthew 6:9; Matthew 5:29: Psalms 37:20
  • The Works, Fruit and Gifts of the Holy Spirit (Healing, Miracles, Signs and Wonders) - John 16:8, 13; Galatians 5:22; 1 Corinthians 12:8-10; Acts 10:38
  • Baptism and Holy Communion (Water by immersion, The Holy Spirit) - Mark 16:16; Mark 1:9-11, John 3:5; Acts 20:7; Mark 14:22-25
  • Eternal as God’s Life (we are to live it right here before we go to heaven) - John 10:10; John 3:16
  • Prosperity and Wealth – 2 Corinthians 8:9; 1 Corinthians 3:21; 3 John 2
  • We are to do the same works and greater works than that which Jesus did. Whatsoever He did, we have the ability to do the same – John 14:10-12
